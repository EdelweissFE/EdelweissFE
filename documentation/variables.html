Variables

Currently, EdelweissFE supports two types of variables: Nodal variables, i.e., fields associated with spatial positions, and scalar variables, not associated with a spatial position. The latter may be used for, e.g., Lagrangian multipliers.

Relevant modules:

  • fe.variables.node

  • fe.variables.scalarvariable

class fe.variables.node.Node(label, coordinates)[source]

A basic node. It has a label, a spatial position, and may be associated with an arbitrary number of fields.

Parameters

  • label (int) – The unique label for this node.

  • coordinates (ndarray) – The coordinates of this node.
